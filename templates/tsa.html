Twitter Sentiment Analysis

Still under development

It is really important for brands, or movie production companies that they understand how their product or movie is doing in the real world, than just accept results off limited number of critics. This twitter sentiment analysis, takes tweets from tweeter related to a product and using machine learning, it output what people actually thing about the product

Technologies Used

  • Scikit Learn
  • Naive bayes
  • tweepy
  • Flask

This website askes user to enter a specific query or a hashtag. This model retrives tweets from tweeter, using twitter api, and performs senitment analysis on them. It then divides the tweets into positive and negative tweets and shows the tweets on the website.
